About Matthew Schlieman

Matt is a dedicated realtor with a strong academic background. After graduating from the University of Minnesota Duluth with degrees in Marketing and Sales, he began decicating all of his time into real estate. Soon after, he completed his real estate licensing courses in 2022 and joined the esteemed North Coast Realty team.

Recognizing the value of trust in his profession, Matt strives to establish meaningful connections with clients and industry peers alike. His personable approach, combined with his fresh knowledge of the real estate market, allows him to address his clients' unique needs and preferences with precision.

Outside of work, Matt cherishes quality time with loved ones, embraces outdoor activities, and passionately supports Minnesota sports. With a commitment to excellence, he looks forward to making your real estate journey a rewarding and successful experience.
